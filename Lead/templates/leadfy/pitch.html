APP SUMMARY

SelfLink is a link platform that helps marketers, brands, influencers, and businesses get insights about the people that clicked on their links.

THREE KINDS OF SERVICES IN A SINGLE APP

SelfLink is social media bio link platform plus an URL shortening service. It also has lead generation capabilities.

FLEXIBLE

SelfLink is flexible. The user decides how he desires to use it based on their needs. It fits the average social media user and the digital marketing specialist.

UNIQUE

We created SelfLink because we know it is annoying not getting some form of contact from the people that visit links. We figured there should be a tool that prompts link visitors to subscribe to the user's email list.

ADVANCED CUSTOMIZATION

SelfLink lets users customize their web page with gradients, selecting background images displayed on both mobile and desktop, choose fonts, sizes, etc.

ADVANCED DASHBOARDS

SelfLink possesses an advanced dashboard that provides statistics such as: - Visitors location - Referral channels - Amount of unique clicks for each link, etc. - Visitors device information - Ability to export different types of information to the corresponding CSV files. - Subscribers' contacts with all the above stats.

SELFLINK IS FAST AND INTUITIVE

SelfLink is a web app but well optimized for mobile devices. It looks like a mobile app. This emphasis on responsiveness and the intuitive and adaptive layout makes SelfLink easy to use.

MONETIZATION

SelfLink is free to use. Premium users have access to interesting additional features such as integrations with other services, more statistics, advanced dashboards, export stats to CSV files, custom URLs, to name a few.

SELFLINK IS AN OPTIMIZED WEB APP WITHOUT COSTS OR RISKS

SelfLink uses the monthly subscription model and, the revenue generated by a single premium user is enough to cover the $5 SelfLink's monthly expenses.
